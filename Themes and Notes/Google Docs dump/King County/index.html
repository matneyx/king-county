    Cities and Towns¶

    • Stanton City
    • Largest city in the region.
    • Based off Grand Junction, CO
    • Shepherd Junction
    • Based off Crescent Junction, UT
    • Basically a truck stop with a street headed toward Jackson  Ridge
    • Two diners
      • One is a Denny's style diner that shares an entrance, building, and parking lot with a truck stop.
      • The other is a run down road house, based off the Roadhouse in Supernatural.
      • Due to the appearance of it's clientele and it's beat up nature, the road house doesn't get much business from people that don't already know what it is.
