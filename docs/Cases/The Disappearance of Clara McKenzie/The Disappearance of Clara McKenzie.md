**Background**: [[Clara McKenzie]] was a local [[Meg's Diner]] waitress who vanished under mysterious circumstances about two years ago in [[King County, Colorado|King County]]. Known for her friendly demeanor, she had recently started speaking about strange occurrences and odd encounters in the area. Her disappearance sparked local interest but faded as time passed without leads. As a regular to Meg's, [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]] knew Clara on a professional level.

**Rudy's Involvement:** An anonymous package arrived at [[Shepherd's Inn]] containing an audio cassette ([[Audio Cassette Transcript]]).

**Leads gathered so far:**
- [[Trish]] (Meg's Diner) pointed Rudy to [[Mike Hastings]], who gave him a current phone number for [[Danny]].
- [[Danny]] confirmed a party in the woods near Brindle the night Clara went missing — she followed a small group (including [[Zack Sawyer]] and an unnamed woman in black) deeper into the trees after overhearing talk of "a sacrifice... a ritual in the woods." She disappeared for good after her next diner shift. [[Amber]] and [[Johnny]] were also at that party and might know exactly where it happened.
- [[Detective Marcus Cole]] identified the neck-tattooed young man as [[Zack Sawyer]] and pulled Danny's old case file — but see the unresolved twist on his own page: his handwriting also turned up on a threatening note at Zack's trailer the same day.
- Rudy went to Zack's trailer and found it ransacked, no sign of Zack, a warning note in what looks like Marcus's hand, and a text thread on Zack's phone with "[[The Broker]]" about wanting "out." [[Amber]] — Zack's close friend, also looking for him — arrived just as Rudy was leaving, and agreed to help investigate.
- The cassette tape itself disappeared from Rudy's truck during a traffic stop with [[Deputy Humboldt]], who — despite being new to the county — knew Rudy's name on sight for no explained reason. He's since turned up loitering around the Brindle service station for weeks, watching for someone or something.
- [[Amber]] and Zack's journal filled in the party-night account: Clara, Zack, and [[Ashley]] followed an unnamed "goth girl" and her crew deeper into the woods. The site is out by [[Raven's Creek]], via the Hollow Path trail — an area the county closed off years ago. Zack's journal entries suggest he kept seeing the goth girl again long after that night, and felt watched.
- [[Johnny]] confirmed rumors of an old cult-meeting-place ruin near the party site, and agreed to guide Rudy and Amber there the next morning.
- A confrontation with [[Deputy Humboldt]] at a church near the service station was defused when [[Detective Marcus Cole|Marcus]] radioed Humboldt away — timing that could be deliberate cover or coincidence.
- Rudy and Amber went to question [[Ashley]] (Zack's cousin) at [[Lucky's Place]] — her interview was in progress, confirming she was part of the group that went into the woods, when the session log cut off.

**Next planned steps (as of the most recent session):** Investigate the ruin at Raven's Creek with Johnny and Amber; finish questioning Ashley; follow up with Marcus about Zack and the debt implied by the warning note.
