Reached via the **Hollow Path** trail, off an old logging road near [[Brindle, Colorado|Brindle]], past a clearing. The county closed the area off years ago after the bridge over the creek rotted out — it's not a place people wander into by accident anymore.

About half a mile past the usual party clearing sits an overgrown ruin — older kids used to tell stories about it being some kind of cult meeting place, never taken seriously until [[Clara McKenzie|Clara]]'s disappearance.

This is where [[Clara McKenzie|Clara]], [[Zack Sawyer|Zack]], and [[Ashley]] followed an unnamed "goth girl" and her crew deeper into the woods the night of the party — the last place Clara was seen before she vanished for good. Not yet investigated by [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]] — planned for the morning after the [[Deputy Humboldt|Humboldt]]/church confrontation, with [[Johnny]] guiding partway and [[Amber]] going all the way in.
