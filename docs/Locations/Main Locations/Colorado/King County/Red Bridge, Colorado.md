> *Based on Olathe, CO*

A small, run-down pass-through town on the Shepherd-Jackson Highway, between [[Shepherd Junction, Colorado|Shepherd Junction]] (north) and [[Jackson Ridge, Colorado|Jackson Ridge]] (south) — the kind of place nobody stops in on purpose, just somewhere you pass through on the way to Jackson Ridge. [[Jensen, Colorado|Jensen]] is a separate town east of Jackson Ridge, off this route.

**Confirmed (2026-07-22, decided live, grill-canon session):** This is the town from [[The Huntsman]]'s Near Miss — [[Christie Reese]] operates out of an old motel here, and it's where [[Jamie|Moss]] sources drugs, hitching a ride out via [[LeGrande Station]]. Resolves the previously-open "drug-run town" question — see [[Development Notes & Unresolved Questions]].
