> *Based on Durango, Colorado*

**Correction (2026-07-22):** A Google Docs town-naming reference Dave rediscovered has Jackson Ridge based on **Durango**, not Cedaredge as this page previously said — Cedaredge is actually [[Hyatt City, Colorado|Hyatt City]]'s basis. The two got swapped somewhere along the way; corrected here to match the source doc.

**Confirmed (2026-07-22):** [[Shepherd Junction, Colorado|Shepherd Junction]] sits on a street headed toward Jackson Ridge — the real-world basis for the already-established "Shepherd-Jackson Highway" naming. You pass through [[Red Bridge, Colorado|Red Bridge]] along the way. [[Jensen, Colorado|Jensen]] is a separate town to the east, off this route.
