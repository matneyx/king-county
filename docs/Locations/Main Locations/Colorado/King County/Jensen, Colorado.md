> *Based on Montrose, CO*

Small town — per [[The Prodigal Son (A King County Chronicle)|The Prodigal Son]]'s Prologue, only a few streetlights mark the intersections of its major roads, and a thick cloud cover can black the place out almost entirely at night. Spelled "Jenson" in that manuscript's prose — not reconciled with the "Jensen" spelling used elsewhere in this vault.
