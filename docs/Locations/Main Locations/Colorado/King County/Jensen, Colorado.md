> *Based on Montrose, CO*

Small town — per [[The Prodigal Son (A King County Chronicle)|The Prodigal Son]]'s Prologue, only a few streetlights mark the intersections of its major roads, and a thick cloud cover can black the place out almost entirely at night. Spelled "Jenson" in that manuscript's prose — not reconciled with the "Jensen" spelling used elsewhere in this vault.

**Notable Locations:**
* [[Love and Sunshine Daycare]]
* [[Western Slope Nursing Care]]

**Not the drug-run town, corrected (2026-07-22, decided live):** Briefly considered as the site of Moss's drug-run motel (see [[The Huntsman]]), but retired — Jensen's based on Montrose, which is too big for what that scene needs. The actual town is meant to be small and run-down, in the vein of Olathe, CO or Wellington, UT — the kind of place you pass through on the way to somewhere that matters. Not yet named; Dave's checking his old Obsidian Portal campaign notes to see if he already logged one. See [[Development Notes & Unresolved Questions]].
