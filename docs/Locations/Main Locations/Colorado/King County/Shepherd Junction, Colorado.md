> ***Based off Crescent Junction, UT***

Basically a truck stop with a street headed toward [[Jackson Ridge, Colorado|Jackson Ridge]]

**Geography (confirmed in [[The Prodigal Son (A King County Chronicle)|The Prodigal Son]]):** Sits at the intersection of US Interstate 70 and Colorado Highway 336, locally known as the Shepherd-Jackson Highway. Consists of a handful of single- and double-wide mobile homes, a small railroad siding with its own warehouse, [[Shepherd's Inn]], and [[LeGrande Station]] directly across the highway.
