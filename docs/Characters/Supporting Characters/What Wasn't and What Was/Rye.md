**"Time went backwards," confirmed as Rye's own phrase, not literal (per "Moss and the Huntsman," full data export):** Rye's own words for what happened to him. "Backwards" is just the only word he had for something that inverted what should've been true: he should be the one who lost the least time, and instead he's the one who lost everything.
