Elderly patient at [[Western Slope Nursing Care]], Room 35 — drooping jowls, forehead wrinkles, the loose double-chin common to the very old. Sleeps through [[Mickey Two-Dice|Mickey]], [[Missy Chamberlain|Missy]], and [[Harv]] bringing [[Allen]] into his room and propping him in the chair, undisturbed even when the lights are switched on. Mickey stands the two old men up to visually compare them for an unstated purpose — see [[The Prodigal Son (A King County Chronicle)]].
