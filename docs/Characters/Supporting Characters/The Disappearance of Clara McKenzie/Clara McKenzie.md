Went missing about two years before [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]]'s investigation reopened the case.

Friends with [[Danny]]
Close friends with [[Trish]]
Worked at [[Meg's Diner]]

Short blonde hair with festive tattoos.

**Personality (per Trish and Danny):** Friendly, eager to help, "had that spark." Not usually one for parties.

**Final known movements:** Attended a party in the woods near Brindle not long before she disappeared, where she overheard talk of "a sacrifice... a ritual in the woods" and followed a small group (including [[Zack Sawyer]] and an unnamed woman) deeper into the trees against [[Danny]]'s advice. Vanished for good after her next shift at Meg's — the same night captured on [[Audio Cassette Transcript|the cassette]] that reopened her case.
