Works at the service station in [[Brindle, Colorado|Brindle]]. Dirty blond hair, usually grease-stained.

Was at the party in the woods the night [[Clara McKenzie|Clara]] disappeared, per [[Danny]] — a possible lead on where exactly that party happened.

## What Actually Happened (played scene)
Wary but willing to talk once [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]] and [[Amber]] approach him at work — asked them to meet him on his smoke break behind the church next to the service station rather than talk on the lot, since [[Deputy Humboldt]] had been hanging around the area "more than usual" for a couple weeks, seemingly watching for something or someone.

Confirmed Zack and Clara were "glued at the hip" most of the party, and that Zack, Clara, and [[Ashley]] went off with a crew of "goth kids" Ashley seemed to know from somewhere. Recalled rumors from older kids about an overgrown ruin about half a mile from the campsite — supposedly an old meeting place for "cults or whatever," never taken seriously until now. Agreed to guide Rudy and Amber out to the site the next morning, but not go in himself.
