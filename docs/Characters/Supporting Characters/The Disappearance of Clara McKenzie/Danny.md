Friend of [[Clara McKenzie]]
Friend of [[Mike Hastings]]
Used to work at [[Meg's Diner]]
Moved to Denver, reportedly hit hard by Clara's disappearance and blaming himself for not stopping her.

Mike gave [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]] Danny's number.

**Phone call with Rudy (2024-11-07):** Confirmed "that night" was a party in the woods near Brindle (bonfire, beer, the usual crowd from Brindle/Jackson Ridge) shortly before Clara vanished. Clara wasn't normally one for parties but insisted on going. Danny saw her wandering off alone, looking over her shoulder like she was being followed; she told him she'd heard people talking about "dark shit — rituals and sacrifices." A small group split off from the party and headed deeper into the woods, and Clara followed them despite Danny trying to talk her out of it. He never saw her again after that night — she disappeared following her next diner shift (the one captured on [[Audio Cassette Transcript|the cassette]]). Danny started seeing shadows himself afterward and still feels watched.

Remembers two unfamiliar faces from the group that split off: a young man he thinks was named "Zack or Zane," with a snake/vine tattoo winding up his neck (see [[Zack Sawyer]]), and a quiet young woman in black who kept her distance from the rest of the party. Named [[Amber]] and [[Johnny]] as two locals who were also at the party and might remember exactly where it happened (near the Brindle trails or out by Red Bridge). Warned Rudy to be careful — "those woods... there's something wrong with them."
