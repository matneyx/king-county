## What Actually Happened (played scene, "Ghosts of King County")
Found by Rudy at [[Zack Sawyer]]'s ransacked trailer, also looking for Zack — she'd come to check on him after he stopped answering his phone and missing work. Wary at first (hand near a gun in her jacket pocket), she warms up once Rudy mentions Danny sent him. Practical, loyal, and not easily spooked: agrees to help investigate despite Rudy's warning that things might get supernatural and that her life "will never be the same" if she comes along. Her answer: "I'm in, Rudy. Weird or not."

**Background with Zack:** Close friends for years — shared a rave by the train yards in Stanton City (~2 years ago) and a drunk hike to Delicate Arch in Moab (last summer). His journal entries about these trips make clear he had unspoken feelings for her, which visibly catches her off guard when Rudy points it out.

**What she knows about the party (the night Clara disappeared):** She was at the party but didn't follow the group into the woods. Confirms it was Zack, Clara, [[Ashley]] (Zack's cousin, close as siblings), and an unnamed "goth girl" (with a crew that hung back at the party's edge, watching but not mixing) who went deeper into the woods together — [[Danny]] went partway too, but came back alone and pale, brushing off questions. Zack came back "real spooked" and stayed jumpy for years after, convinced someone or something was watching him. Says the site was out by **Raven's Creek**, reached via the **Hollow Path** trail off an old logging road, past a clearing — an area the county closed off years ago after the bridge over the creek rotted out.

**Zack's journal (found in his room):** Two entries stand out — "Saw her again tonight. She looked… wrong. But she knew things. Things no one should know about that night in the woods. Said they'd come for me if I didn't listen." and "It's not just whispers anymore. Shadows, moving where they shouldn't. I'm starting to wonder if Danny was right. Maybe we didn't leave all of them behind that night." No dates, but the second entry is more recent (references the Moab trip).

**Current plan:** Agreed to go with Rudy to talk to [[Johnny]] and then [[Ashley]] at [[Lucky's Place]], and to lead Rudy out to the party site the next morning (with [[Johnny]] guiding partway) rather than go into the woods at night.

Carries a gun and says she'll use it if it comes to protecting Zack or herself — visibly nervous about that prospect but not backing down.
