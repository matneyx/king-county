Unseen contact, saved in [[Zack Sawyer|Zack Sawyer's]] phone as "The Broker." Deals in information, smuggling, and silence — for a price. Described by [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]] as more myth than known figure in the darker circles he's aware of.

Last message to Zack, sent the night his trailer was ransacked and he went missing: "If you still want out, tonight's the night. Bring the cash, and don't tell a soul."

**Confirmed connection to Marcus Cole:** Detective [[Detective Marcus Cole|Marcus Cole]] is indebted to The Broker through an "Old Deal" struck sometime in the past — the exact nature of the debt is unknown, but it's serious enough that it's pushed Marcus to act against his own moral compass, which explains (at least in part) the warning note in his handwriting found at Zack's trailer. Whether The Broker is directly involved in Clara's or Zack's disappearance, or just adjacent to it through Marcus, is still unresolved. See [[Development Notes & Unresolved Questions]].

Operates through debt and leverage rather than force — keeps a network of people obligated to them through secrets and old favors. Reputation alone is usually enough to keep people in line.
