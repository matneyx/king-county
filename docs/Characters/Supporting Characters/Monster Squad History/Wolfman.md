The [[California Werewolf Pack]] member blood-bound to [[The Vampire]] during [[The California Incident]] — brainstormed mechanics for how a "shot and killed" werewolf from the film actually plays out under World of Darkness rules.

**Nature:** Tribeless — a Ghost Wolf — afflicted with a Flaw that forces an involuntary Change tied to the moon's phase.

**The police-station incident:** Rather than a random rampage, he went to the police specifically to be locked up for the night, trying not to hurt anyone. He Changed, was shot, and was presumed dead — but werewolves regenerate from anything short of aggravated damage, so he actually revived in the ambulance and escaped, killing the driver in the process. This is the "missing body, dead ambulance driver" incident [[Sean]] reports to the rest of the club as an early sign something's wrong.

**Capture:** [[The Vampire]] got to him after his escape — in this version, not drugged as in the film, but blood-bound outright. He never broke free of the bond.

**Death:** Present for the final battle largely as depicted in the film, including [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]] landing the killing blow — either Rudy kills him outright, or simply gets the last shot in with a silver bullet, which causes aggravated damage a werewolf can't shrug off.
