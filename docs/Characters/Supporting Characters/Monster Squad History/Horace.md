A neighborhood kid who befriended [[Rudy Williams|Rudy]] over the summer before either of them started school together — round-faced, easygoing, never treated Rudy like the "dangerous new kid" everyone else assumed he was. Rudy didn't hear the other kids call him "Fat Kid" until weeks after they'd already become friends, and it pissed him off when he did.

Invited Rudy into the [[The Monster Squad (group)|Monster Club]] right as the school year started — nervous about it, half-expecting to be laughed at.

**Confirmed scenes:**
- Got in trouble with [[Sean]] in the principal's office for drawing monster pictures in science class — Rudy, despite never actually having been in trouble himself before moving, talked Horace down with an invented line about permanent records: "It doesn't matter. Your permanent record is a myth. I've been to the principal's office hundreds of times, and none of that stuff followed me here."
- Present with Rudy in the Vampire's mansion when Wolfman was there — kicked Wolfman in the nards.
- **His level-up moment**, fighting off one of the Vampire's brides: cocking a shotgun and declaring, "My name," he says, cocking the shotgun in his hands, "is Horace!"

**Fate:** Like the rest of the Monster Club, presumed dead.
