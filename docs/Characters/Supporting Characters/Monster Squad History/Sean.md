[[Phoebe]]'s older brother and the actual leader of the [[The Monster Squad (group)|Monster Club]] — Rudy is emphatically not the one who ran things; that was always Sean.

**Personality:** Bold, always chasing the next big scheme (sneaking into the supposedly-haunted Silverstein house, watching bootleg horror movies in his basement), the type who drags everyone else along for the ride without a second thought. Protective of Phoebe in practice even while constantly telling her to go home and stop following them.

**Cop's kid:** His father is a police officer, which is how he's first to hear about the break-in shooting at the station and the missing body/dead ambulance driver — the group's first real sign that something in town is wrong (see [[The California Incident]]).

**Fate, deliberately left open (2026-07-22, decided live):** Not resolved on purpose — unlike [[Horace]], whose fate is now confirmed mundane, Dave wants Sean's ending to stay undecided for now.

**Falling out with Rudy, confirmed (2026-07-22, decided live):** Sean stayed invested in what happened longer than Horace did, and may have gotten more involved in his own way over time — but he and Rudy couldn't keep working together, purely down to personality conflict, not any external force driving them apart. Part of the same gradual, spread-out drift that cost Rudy the rest of the group before he ever joined the Marines.
