The fae realm. Not a vacuum — it's obsessed with mirroring, mocking, and manipulating the real world. The Gentry steal people, ideas, language, objects, and culture, not to preserve them, but to repurpose them for their own alien aesthetics.

"Arcadia is like Hell with a scrapbook fetish: it doesn't just steal from the real world, it remixes it in the worst way possible."

Time is warped there — a Changeling can spend centuries in Arcadia while barely aging, and glimpses of the real world can filter in asynchronously (a cracked mirror, a bleeding television set, secondhand stories filtered through fae nonsense).

[[Jamie]] was taken into Arcadia early enough in life to not remember life before, and was held there for centuries.
