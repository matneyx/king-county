## Notes
---
Rudy calls [[Detective Marcus Cole|Marcus Cole]] to ask about the deputy who worked his deer-collision scene the night before — turns out to be [[Deputy Humboldt]], new to the county, transferred from Nevada. Marcus is unsettled that Humboldt knew Rudy's name. Rudy also confirms the cassette (tied to [[Clara McKenzie]]'s case) went missing during that stop.

Rudy calls [[Danny]] directly. Danny confirms "that night" was a party in the woods near Brindle, describes Clara following a small group — including a young man with a neck tattoo and an unfamiliar woman in black — deeper into the trees, and gives up two more names who were there: [[Amber]] (bartender, Lucky's Place, Jackson Ridge) and [[Johnny]] (service station, Brindle).

Rudy calls Marcus back with the tattoo description; Marcus IDs the young man as [[Zack Sawyer]] and sends his address in Brindle.

**Original Mythic rolls for this Turning Point** (before a re-roll produced "Altered Scene," discarded): DEAD; CHARACTER EXITS THE ADVENTURE; A CHARACTER ACTS OUT OF CHARACTER; AN OLD DEAL. Initial interpretation (not used): Rudy arrives to find Zack dead, killed by Marcus to fulfill an old debt, with a note reading "No one was supposed to get this far. I'm sorry. My debt is paid." Replaced by the version below when the scene was rerolled as "Altered."

**What actually happened:** Rudy arrives at Zack's single-wide trailer in Brindle to find it ransacked — drawers emptied, no sign of a struggle so much as a hurried search. A note taped to the wall, in handwriting Rudy immediately recognizes as [[Detective Marcus Cole|Marcus]]'s: *"This should've ended years ago, Rudy. You're not supposed to know any of this. Leave it alone, or someone else will end up in the ground."* Zack's phone, screen cracked but still on, shows a text thread with a contact saved as "[[The Broker]]" — the last message reading *"If you still want out, tonight's the night. Bring the cash, and don't tell a soul."* A clock in the trailer is running roughly three hours slow, for no explained reason.

Rudy pockets the note and the phone and heads for the door as a car pulls up outside — a young woman, mid-20s, choppy black hair under a ball cap, a gun-shaped bulge in her jacket pocket, also looking for Zack. She's [[Amber]], one of Zack's oldest friends and a bartender at [[Lucky's Place]].

# Turning Point: 4 (Amber's Introduction and Zack's Journal)
## Notes
Rudy and Amber size each other up outside the trailer; she opens up once he mentions [[Danny]] sent him. She confirms the party-night account: Clara, Zack, and [[Ashley]] followed an unnamed "goth girl" and her crew deeper into the woods, toward a site out by [[Raven's Creek]] via the Hollow Path trail — county-closed for years since the bridge over the creek rotted out.

A search of Zack's room turns up his journal (no other clues — Mythic rolled "Exceptional No" twice). Two entries stand out: "Saw her again tonight. She looked… wrong. But she knew things..." and "It's not just whispers anymore. Shadows, moving where they shouldn't. Maybe we didn't leave all of them behind that night." Amber is visibly caught off guard realizing Zack's entries about their shared history (a rave in Stanton City, a Moab hiking trip) suggest he had feelings for her.

Rudy warns Amber what she's signing up for if she keeps helping — that this may be genuinely supernatural, and there's no going back to treating him as "just a crazy old man" once she's seen it. She chooses to stay in anyway: "I'm in, Rudy. Weird or not."

They agree to talk to [[Johnny]] next, then [[Ashley]].

# Turning Point: 5 (Johnny, the Church, and Deputy Humboldt)
## Mythic rolls
RELIGION; SAVIOR ([[Detective Marcus Cole|Marcus Cole]]); PROTECTOR ([[Johnny]], protecting Rudy/Amber); SOMEONE IS WHERE THEY SHOULD NOT BE ([[Deputy Humboldt]]). Interruption Event, NPC-negative, "Divide" and "War."

## Notes
Johnny, wary of talking on the service station lot, asks Rudy and Amber to meet him on his smoke break behind the church next door — he's noticed [[Deputy Humboldt]] loitering around that same lot "more than usual" for a couple of weeks, like he's watching for someone or something.

Before Johnny can join them, Humboldt shows up at the church, needling Rudy about "showing up where trouble's brewing." Confirmed in-scene: Rudy is 5'7", Humboldt roughly 6'1". Marcus radios Humboldt away to a fabricated(?) call in Stockton before it escalates — timing that could be Marcus running interference for Rudy, or genuine coincidence.

**GM framing for Humboldt's motive (not yet dramatized):** tied to Rudy's time in the Marines — implies Rudy did something that divided Humboldt's family.

Johnny then confirms Zack, Clara, and Ashley went off with the goth-kid crew, and recalls rumor of an old ruin about half a mile past the usual campsite — a supposed cult meeting place, dismissed as kid talk until now. Agrees to guide Rudy and Amber to the site the next morning, but won't go in himself. Plan: investigate Raven's Creek in daylight, not that night.

## Notes
Rudy and Amber go to [[Lucky's Place]] to find [[Ashley]], Zack's cousin — guarded, sharp-eyed, clearly expecting this conversation. She confirms she, Zack, and the goth-kid crew went back into the woods that night, framing it as "just a good party" at first. Session log ends mid-interview.
